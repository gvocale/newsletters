Introducing Fr8 Network

 

A new paradigm for the freight industry.

 

We’re building the efficiency layer to solve today’s challenges and power the future of freight - from AI and automation to self driving trucks.
Visit our website

HOW IT WORKS

 

Get matched to the best shipper or carrier in seconds.

 

The $700B domestic freight industry lacks any cohesive, trusted solution creating a lack of transparency and preventing market evolution.

Fr8 Network is a set of decentralized applications designed to connect the freight industry’s fractured value chain by transparently connecting key stakeholders, removing high-cost arbitrage, and redistributing revenue.

Our blockchain network facilitates the digitization of record-keeping related to the trade of assets, even in scenarios where intermediaries and brokers are incentivized to resist change.

DECENTRALIZED


Decentralization prevents intermediaries from controlling reputations and price discovery, fueling market efficiencies.

TRANSPARENT


Fr8 breaks down walled gardens and enables data to be used programmatically, driving value in an open, free, and fair marketplace.

SELF-REGULATING


Using a proprietary algorithm, Fr8 combines historical performance with peer reviews to generate a rating for every user, creating strong incentive alignment.



OUR MISSION

 

Building for today, to support tomorrow

 

We believe in creating something real for immediate adoption.

Disintermediating the supply chain is a big, challenging - but worthy - goal. And there’s no shortage of blockchain companies doing huge ICOs to support massive development plans. We’re different.

At Fr8, we believe it’s important to have big goals - but we also think it’s important to deliver value quickly. So while we may have some similar BHAG goals, we plan to ship products iteratively, delivering value to both users and token holders in both the short and long term.
